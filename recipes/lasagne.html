i n g r e d i e n t s

the ragu:

  • 2 Tbsp of Olive Oil
  • 900g of Minced Beef
  • 2 Large Onions
  • 2 Sticks of Celery
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • 2 Tbsp of Plain or All
    Purpose Flour
  • 150ml of Beef Stock
  • 1 Tsp of Caster Sugar
  • 3 Tbsp of Tomato Puree
  • 1 Tbsp of Chopped
    Thyme
  • 2 Cans of Chopped
    Tomatoes

the bechamel:

  • 50g Butter
  • 50g Plain or All Purpose
    Flour
  • 750ml of Milk

the lasagne:

  • Lasagne Sheets
  • Cheddar Cheese

m e t h o d


  1. Heat a large frying pan until hot and add the
    oil.
  2. Cook the mince until until beef is fully
    cooked and slightly crisp.
  3. Remove from heat, cover and keep it warm.
  4. In that same pan, add some chopped onion, celery, and
    garlic and cook until softened.
  5. Before returning the meat, add some tomato puree and
    thyme to the softened onion and stir.
  6. Combine the meat and stock to the frying pan.
  7. Add some sugar to the meat and stir in the canned
    tomatoes and bring to a boil.
  8. Once boiling, bring the heat down to a simmer.
  9. For the bechamel, melt butter in a saucepan.
  10. Add flour and cook over the heat for one minute.
  11. Gradually pour in some milk and whisk until fully combined.
  12. Once thickened, add salt and pepper to taste.
  13. Preheat the oven to 200C/ 400F/ Gas 6.
  14. For the lasagne, layer the ragu, pasta sheets, bechamel,
    and cheese leaving the last layer as bechamel.
  15. Cover the dish with aluminum and cook in the middle of
    the oven for 45 minutes.
  16. When the pasta sheets are soft, take it out and top with
    cheese.
  17. Return to the oven until the cheese is golden and browned.
  18. Once cooked, allow to cool and then enjoy!
