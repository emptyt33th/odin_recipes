Tomato and Mozzarella Salad

Home

m e t h o d

  1. Whisk the oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
  2. Toss the rocket with 1 tbsp of the vinaigrette in a another bowl.
  3. Place the dressed rocket on a platter.
  4. Decoratively arrange sliced tomatoes and burrata over the rocket.
  5. Sprinkle the basil and some flaky salt.
  6. Enjoy!

Orange and Rocket

Fresh and Fruity with Some Bite

Home

m e t h o d

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the greens, pomegranate seeds, walnuts, and peeled mandarins.
  2. Separately, whisk together olive oil, vinegar, mustard, honey, salt, and pepper.
  3. Toss the salad and herbs with this dressing.
  4. Top with a little pomegranate.
  5. Break apart some feta cheese and sprinkle on the salad.
  6. Enjoy!
