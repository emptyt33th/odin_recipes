Tomato and Mozzarella Salad

Home





Here is the recipe that inspired this:


i n g r e d i e n t s


  • 2 Cups of Rocket
  • 4 Cups of Plum
    Tomatoes
  • 1 Ball of Burrata
    Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup of Basil
  • 2 Tbsp of Extra-Virgin
    Olive Oil
  • 1 Tbsp of Balsamic
    Vinegar
  • 1/4 Tsp of Salt
  • 1/4 Tsp of Ground Pepper
  • 1/4 Tsp of Flaky Sea Salt


m e t h o d

  1. Whisk the oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper in a
    small bowl.
  2. Toss the rocket with 1 tbsp of the
    vinaigrette in a another bowl.
  3. Place the dressed rocket on a platter.
  4. Decoratively arrange sliced tomatoes and burrata
    over the rocket.
  5. Sprinkle the basil and some flaky salt.
  6. Enjoy!

Fresh and Fruity with Some Bite





Here is the inspiration for this recipe:


i n g r e d i e n t s



  • 2 Bunches of Rocket
  • 1 Bunch of Mint
  • 1 Bunch of Coriander
  • 1 Bunch of Spring Salad
    Mix
  • 1 Cup of Peeled
    Mandarins
  • 1 Cup of Pomegranate
    Seeds
  • 1/2 Cup of Walnuts
  • 1/3 Cup of Feta Cheese
  • 3 Tsp of Olive Oil
  • 2 Tsp of Vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp of Honey
  • 1 1/2 Tsp of Dijon
    Mustard
  • Salt
  • Pepper


m e t h o d



  1. In a large bowl, mix together the greens,
    pomegranate seeds, walnuts, and peeled
    mandarins.
  2. Separately, whisk together olive oil, vinegar,
    mustard, honey, salt, and pepper.
  3. Toss the salad and herbs with this dressing.
  4. Top with a little pomegranate.
  5. Break apart some feta cheese and sprinkle on
    the salad.
  6. Enjoy!
